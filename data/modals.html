Mystara Arena

Designer of the project Mystara Arena. It is a game based on the arcade classic Shadows over Mystara and it was made in C++ with SDL. Mystara Arena is a college project game that has been developed over the course in the subject Project II by our team, NOREADME Studios. The objective of this project was to create a fully featured game that could be learned in around 10 to 20 minutes and that could create a competitive environment for a group of friends to try and challenge each other in the 1vs1 and 2vs2 game modes, while ensuring a very fun gameplay. We have taken most of the art and sounds from the arcade game "Dungeons and Dragons: Shadow over Mystara", a beat'em up game released by Capcom in 1996.

The Heist

Prototype of a 3D level for a game concept made in Unreal Engine.
The main story of the level and the game is that the main character has to enter the minds of people to steal memories of information. The mind of the host is represented in a surrealistic environment where the memory to steal is a physical object. To reinforce the surrealistic mood, the closer to the object the smaller the player is. A one page dungeon made by Michael Prescott and is part of the Trilemma Adventures one page dungeons collection.

The Non Euclidean Shoot

Prototype of a 3D level for a game concept made in Unreal Engine.
The Non-Euclidean Shoot is a first person shooter where the player has to cross an intergalactic port. But this port is a usual station, it’s located inside a dark hole, therefore in this place nothing is what it seems. This map is a non-euclidean space, that means that space bends and the laws of the universe are broken.
